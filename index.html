Bitcoin Knots is a Bitcoin full node implementation
with option to manage multiple wallets and better UI/UX.

Features:
  • You hold your own bitcoins
  • Full verification of payments
  • Easy to send and receive
  • Backup your wallet just like saving a document
  • Optional expert control for advanced users
  • Pair with your phone wallet to upgrade its security

Security notice

Please note: When installing this or any other app, you should verify your download is the authentic program, and not intercepted by a virus. Always verify the correct digital signature on any downloads to avoid malware. Bitcoin Knots is signed by Luke Dashjr's OpenPGP key. You should ideally download the key from at least two independent sources and ensure they match. Luke's key is also available from bitcoin.org. Click here for a tutorial on how to verify the OpenPGP signature!

If the digital signature is missing, broken, or fails to verify, do not trust the downloaded files, and contact Luke Dashjr immediately.
